About Us

Welcome to SA Music Tuition!

We’re a passionate team of experienced music teachers offering personalized music lessons right at your doorstep. Our goal is to make learning music convenient, fun, and affordable, all from the comfort of your own home.

Here at SA Music Tuition, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to explore their musical talents, no matter their age or skill level. Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced musician, we strive to deliver quality lessons that are made for you.

Join us at SA Music Tuition and experience the joy of learning music in a way that’s flexible, personalized, and affordable. We can’t wait to help you reach your musical goals!

Our Services

30 Minute Lessons

Perfect for those starting at a young age, 30 minute lessons are ideal for inspiring young ones and fuelling their passion for music. 30 minute lessons focus on developing simple, fundamental knowledge of the instrument though practical learning.

45 Minute Lessons

45 minute lessons are the perfect step up for those looking to take their music to the next level, allowing more time for the students musicality to be developed. Focusing still on practical learning, this option also allows for more technical and theoretical concepts to be developed in lessons.

1 Hour Lessons

Full hour lessons are the desired lesson type for students striving to achieve their best in their musical endeavours. 1 hour lessons allow time for all aspects of music to be covered adequately in class, remaining focused on both developing the student’s skills on their instrument, but also curating their passion and love for music.
